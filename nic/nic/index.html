Born in the Heart of Portland.
Cathedral’s journey in the garden started years ago as a tiny medical grow room in our office. Since then, we have grown into a 200-light, award-winning operation featured in HIGH TIMES (RIP City Mega Grow). And thus, Cathedral was born.
Cathedral embodies the independent and grassroots spirit of the St. John's neighborhood in Portland. We specialize in high-quality genetics and flowers cultivated from the most sought-after and renowned strains, many of which we breed ourselves.
As a small team, we take a hands-on approach to every aspect of the process, from mixing and pruning to harvesting and trimming. This results in a truly artisanal product from beginning to end.

Our story

Just like the St.Johns Bridge, Cathedral has evolved into an enduring symbol of Portland and a reminder of the city's gritty industrial history.
